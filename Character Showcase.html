Cypher is a sentinel agent from the first person shooter game valorant. He is the 5th agent to join and acts as a spy and information broker for the valorant protocol. He is of morroccan decent and relies on his abilities to ambush and catch the enemy off guard. “No secret is safe and no maneuver unseen as Cypher is always watching.”

BACKSTORY

Amir El Amari (Arabic: أمير العماري meaning "prince of the moon") more known as Cypher, is from Rabat Morocco. The town that he grew up in was depleted of resources by Kingdom Corporation which resulted in his hate for them. Cypher used to work alone before being recruited by the Valorant Protocol and seem to have lost his family but occasionally will mention someone named Nora. As an information broker, he gathers intel and keeps to himself being especially careful about his own information. Cypher is never seen without his black mask covering his face and has multiple safe houses in order to remain in the dark as much as possible.
